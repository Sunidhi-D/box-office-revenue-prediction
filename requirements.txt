Hardware:
 • A modern computer with a multi-core processor.
 • At least 8GB of RAM.
 • Sufficient storage for datasets and model checkpoints (preferably SSD).
Software:
 • Operating System: Windows, macOS, or Linux.
 • Programming Language: Python.
 • Libraries: pandas, numpy, scikit-learn, tensorflow/keras (for neural networks), matplotlib, seaborn.
 • Development Environment: Jupyter Notebook or any other IDE like PyCharm
